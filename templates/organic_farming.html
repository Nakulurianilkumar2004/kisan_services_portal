Organic Farming

What is Organic Farming?

Organic farming is an agricultural practice that promotes the use of natural substances and processes to grow crops and raise livestock. It focuses on sustainability, biodiversity, and soil health without relying on synthetic chemicals.

Benefits of Organic Farming

  • Enhances soil fertility and biodiversity.
  • Eliminates the use of harmful pesticides and synthetic fertilizers.
  • Produces healthier and more nutritious food.
  • Reduces environmental pollution and promotes sustainability.
  • Supports animal welfare and ethical farming practices.
