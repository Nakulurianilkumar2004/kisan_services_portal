Importance of Soil Testing in Farming

  • Soil testing helps determine the nutrient levels and pH of the soil, essential for healthy crop growth.
  • It aids in identifying nutrient deficiencies, ensuring the right fertilizers are used to boost yield.
  • Soil testing helps in reducing excessive fertilizer usage, which saves costs and protects the environment.
  • Improves soil management practices by understanding the soil's physical and chemical properties.
  • Ensures long-term soil health and sustainability for future farming activities.
